Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

JOBSpan ("we," "our," "us") is a job management, estimating, and invoicing platform operated by TDX Systems and Consulting for use by contracting businesses, including 7 Pillars Contracting. This Privacy Policy explains what information JOBSpan collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Collect

JOBSpan stores information that users (contractors and their staff) enter directly into the application, including:

  • Customer names, contact information, and property addresses
  • Job, estimate, invoice, and payment records
  • Team member names, roles, and login information
  • Vendor and subcontractor information

How We Use Information

Information stored in JOBSpan is used solely to provide the job management, estimating, invoicing, and scheduling features of the application. We do not sell user or customer data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

JOBSpan integrates with the following third-party services to provide its functionality:

  • Google Firebase — used for data storage and user authentication
  • QuickBooks Online (Intuit) — used, at the user's option, to sync invoices for accounting and payment collection. JOBSpan only accesses QuickBooks data necessary to create and reference invoices. JOBSpan does not process payments directly — payment collection, when used, is handled entirely by Intuit's own QuickBooks Payments service.

Each of these providers maintains its own privacy practices, which govern their handling of data on our behalf.

Data Security

Data is stored using Google Firebase's Firestore database with access controls limiting data to authorized users within each contracting business. QuickBooks account credentials (OAuth tokens) are stored securely and are never exposed to end users or transmitted outside of JOBSpan's backend infrastructure.

Data Retention

Job, estimate, and invoice records are retained as long as the associated business account remains active, or as required for legitimate business and accounting purposes.

Your Choices

Users may disconnect JOBSpan's QuickBooks integration at any time from within QuickBooks' own connected-apps settings, or by contacting us directly.
